Research Symposium in Mechanical Engineering (RSME) 2022

The Department of Mechanical Engineering organised ‘Research Symposium in Mechanical Engineering (RSME) 2022’, a national-level research presentation, to give researchers a platform to present their ongoing research activities. The event included keynote lectures from expert faculty members and industry personnel. 
 

14th GRIHA Summit

IIT Bhilai won the 5-star GRIHA rating award in ‘Master Plan Large Development’ at the 14th GRIHA summit. The event was held in New Delhi, with this year’s theme being ‘Towards Net Positive Habitats’.  

Inter-IIT Sports Meet 2022

Students from IIT Bhilai can be seen taking part in the Inter-IIT Sports Meet 2022. Badminton: IIT Bhilai vs. IIT Bombay; Table Tennis: IIT Bhilai vs. IIT Roorkee.At the Inter-IIT Sports Meet 2022, IIT Bhilai students secured the 4th rank in march-past.
